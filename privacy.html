Best Western Ariel Hotel ("we", "us", "our") operates the Airport Parking service at London Heathrow. We are the data controller for personal data collected through this parking management system.

3. How we use your data

  • To manage your parking booking from check-in to collection
  • To allocate and track your vehicle's parking space
  • To calculate and process parking charges
  • To send booking confirmation and receipts by email (where provided)
  • To resolve any disputes about vehicle condition
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely in the European Union (EU West region) using Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database provider. Vehicle photos are stored in secure cloud storage. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 1. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

Our parking management system stores the following data in your browser's local storage (not cookies):

  • Your login session token (staff system only)
  • System configuration settings

No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used. The guest check-in form does not use any cookies or local storage.

8. CCTV

Our car park areas are monitored by CCTV for the safety and security of vehicles and guests. CCTV footage is retained for 30 days and may be shared with law enforcement if required.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated. Continued use of the parking service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
